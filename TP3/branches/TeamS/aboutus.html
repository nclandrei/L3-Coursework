About Us
The Regional Observatory

is all about understanding our region by making information openly available to all whether you are a business, householder, student, visitor or professional.

We want Dumfries and Galloway, and the South of Scotland as a whole, to be a better informed area with a sense of 'place' to help project ourselves on the national and international stage.

A great starting point to discover key information on Dumfries and Galloway is by looking at the Baseline Study and Regional Economic Profile for Dumfries and Galloway Regional Economic Strategy 2014-2020, which is available through this website. We also carry all four area profiles on this website. Please have a look at the Annandale and Eskdale Local Area Profile, the Nithsdale Local Area Profile, the Stewartry Local Area Profile and the Wigtownshire Local Area Profile for more detailed information.

Underpinning to the collaborative work that has taken place to build the Observatory site are the two Single Outcome Agreements: Dumfries and Galloway and Scottish Borders.
